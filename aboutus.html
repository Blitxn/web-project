About Us

Cakey is your one-stop online platform to order cakes from a wide range of local bakeries. We make it easy for you to explore, choose, and pick up delicious cakes from trusted stores in your area.

Our mission is to connect customers with high-quality bakeries and simplify the cake ordering experience. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or special event, Cakey ensures you can browse, customize, and schedule your pickup—all online.

We partner with local bakeries to bring you the freshest, most delightful cakes while giving you the convenience of online ordering and real-time availability.
